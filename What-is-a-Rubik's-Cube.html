What is a Rubik's Cube

A Rubik's Cube, originaly called the Magic Cube, is a cube with dimensions of 3x3x3. The objective is to make it so that all six sides are the same color.

This may sound easy, but all you can do to achive this goal is spinning the six sides. If you don't have a cube yet you can buy them on Amazon or at some stores.

The shape of Rubix Cubes may vary, and not all cubes are 3x3x3. For example, there is a 4x4x4, a 5x5x5, and even a pyramid.
